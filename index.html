03 // PROJECTS

Featured Work

PROJECT_001 · IoT
OTA Firmware Update Service

Part of the team that designed and built a cloud-native OTA (Over-the-Air) update microservice managing firmware OTA updates for over 3+ million IoT devices globally. The service handles firmware image pooling, versioned bundle management, and rule-based release targeting — orchestrating updates across a large heterogeneous device fleet on GCP. A key area of focus has been observability: the service exposes real-time health and throughput metrics via Micrometer and Prometheus, with Grafana dashboards providing visibility into update pipeline status, message delivery health, and system availability at scale.
